560 CE, Northern & Southern Dynasties (3)

534 After rebellions by the north Xianbei troops against sinicisation of the kingdom, the Northern Wei empire splits. Gao Huan rules the Eastern Wei, and forcibly removes the inhabitants of Luoyand to Ye, which can be better defended. Yuwen Tai rules the Western Wei.

551 The Eastern Wei become the Northern Qi.

557 The Western Wei become the Northern Zhou.
