560 CE, Northern & Southern Dynasties (4)

551 Gao Yang forces the emperor to yield the throne to him and the Eastern Wei become the Northern Qi.

The Western Wei attacks the Northern Qi, but fails to conquer and loses a few provinces to them.

553 The Western Wei conquer the western provinces of the Liang dynasty.

554 The Western Wei capture Jiangling (Jingzhou, Hubei), the Liang dynasty capital and execute the Liang emperor.

557 The Western Wei become the Northern Zhou.

Chen Baxian takes power from the Liang emperor and founds the Chen dynasty.

577 The Northern Zhou defeat the Northern Qi, reunifying the north.

581 Yang Jian overthrows the Northern Zhou and becomes the first Sui emperor.
